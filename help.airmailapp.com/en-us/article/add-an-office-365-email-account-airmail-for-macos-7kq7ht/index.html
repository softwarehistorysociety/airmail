Add an Office 365 Email Account - Airmail for macOS

Add Office365 account with Modern Authentication using the Office365 option present in the Airmail app.


Go to "Add your email account" window and then choose Office365 option.
Enter the details of your account in the window.
And then click on "Add".






How to add Office365 accounts as Exchange account.


An Office 365 (office365) account can also be added as an Microsoft Exchange Account. To add an O365 account,
follow these steps

Go to "Add your email account" window and then choose Exchange option.
Enter the details of your account in the window.
And then click on "Add".


If ask to enter Server info use the domain name in Server column.
For example, in above case you have to use airmlr.com as server field



If ask to enter the Endpoint URL of account, please use
https://outlook.office365.com/EWS/Exchange.asmx as URL for Office 365 account.
